What's it about?

Lampedia is a website made by me that is updated every week with a lamp of my choosing from the internet that I believe to be worthy of showcasing for a week. I do not have a rating system nor have any bias it's purely my opinion only. I do sometimes present a lamp of choosing from one of my friends or my partner. I do not do this to advertise for companies or am I in any way sponsored by any of these companies.

What gave you the idea?

The idea of Lampedia was brought upon a joke between me, my partner and some teammates in VALORANT. I recall a teammate shouting out the word "Lamp" resulting in some confusion as I had not understood that call out on the map we were currently playing on. The following rounds resulted in everyone joking around about the call out and amongst the laughter the idea of creating a website purely around the idea of Lamps was brought to life. I in my determination to continue the joke to extreme lengths went about creating the website and had built the first idea in a matter of hours due to my already extensive knowledge in website building using HTML and CSS due to my studies.
